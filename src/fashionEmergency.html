Fashion Emergency

Emergency: Stained Outfit

Your favorite outfit just got stained right before an important event. What do you do?

Fix: Act quickly! Blot the stain gently with a clean cloth and apply a stain remover. If possible, change into a different outfit for the event.

Emergency: Broken Heel

Your high heel just broke while you're out. How can you save your style?

Fix: Find a nearby store and purchase a pair of comfortable flats or sandals as a quick replacement. Consider carrying a foldable pair of emergency shoes in your bag for future mishaps.
